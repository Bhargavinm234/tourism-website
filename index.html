About Mysuru

Mysuru, formerly known as Mysore, is one of South India's most culturally vibrant cities. Known for its majestic palaces, rich traditions, and vibrant festivals like Dasara, it attracts travelers from around the world. Explore the beauty, history, and elegance of this royal city.

Mysore Palace

The grand royal residence of the Wadiyar dynasty and a jewel of Indo-Saracenic architecture.

Mysore Zoo

One of India's oldest zoos, housing rare and exotic species in naturalistic enclosures.

Chamundi Hills

Visit the sacred Chamundeshwari Temple located atop the serene Chamundi Hills.

Brindavan Gardens

Famous for its musical fountain and symmetrical landscaping, a visual treat for tourists.

Venugopala Swamy Temple

Temple by the dam backwaters providing a serene setting for meditation, with cultural significance and historic architecture.

Melkote

Known for Yoga Narasimha temple and beautiful hill views. A peaceful spiritual destination.

Namdroling Monastery

A stunning Tibetan monastery with golden architecture, offering a calm and spiritual experience.

Lokaranjan Aqua Zoo

Devaraja Market

Historic market full of color, fragrance, and energy. Great for flowers, spices, and photos!

St. Philomena's Church

Neo-Gothic cathedral known for its towering architecture and peaceful ambience for meditation.

Mysore Sand Sculpture Museum

Sand sculpture gallery showcasing intricate artistry with 150 detailed pieces themed around Indian mythology and world legends, set in a creative space.

Avadhoota Datta Peetham

A renowned spiritual center founded by Sri Ganapathy Sachchidananda Swamiji, promoting moral, cultural, and spiritual values.
